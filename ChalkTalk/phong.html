Phong Lighting Explained


The world according to computer graphics it that the +X-axis is to the left, the +Y-axis is up, and looking into the computer screen, is the -Z-axis.
The +Z-axis comes out of the screen towards your eyeballs.


So this is a side view. Since I'm looking left the -X-axis is pointing toward you.
Let's define your eye to be at the origin, looking into the screen is -Z space where everything is happening.
From your eye, you are looking looking through your monitor out into a mathamatical universe.
That universe is projected onto the computer screen by our software for you to view and enjoy.


There are three priciple (matrix) transforms that must be performed in order order to project objects onto the screen.
The are the ModelMatrix, the ViewMatrix and the ProjectionMatrix.
